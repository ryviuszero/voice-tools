Hermes is a white-label operating platform for agencies that deploy AI voice
agents for their clients. It bundles voice agent deployment, a built-in CRM,
campaign orchestration for inbound and outbound calling, and transparent
usage billing into one platform that runs under the agency's own brand,
replacing the separate voice API, CRM, automation, billing, and telephony
tools agencies usually stitch together. Plans start at $149/month with 3
client workspaces and 300 included minutes; voice usage beyond included
minutes is a flat $0.24 per minute.
